    Our Story

    Our journey has evolved from humble beginnings. We survived on a bumper
    road before making Tiggle available directly to your doorstep. How?

    Farm and Team


    No longer a one-man army, we partnered with Tamil Nadu farmers for top-quality cocoa and skilled women in Agra to produce fresh Tiggle packets. Our virtual office is filled with awesome folks.

    On the streets


    The journey began with a simple idea and a burning desire to do something unconventional. Every evening after work, we sold our freshly made hot chocolate outside a metro station in Delhi. Crazy!

    30 cups a day


    After weeks of street selling, we partnered with kiosks but only sold 30-40 cups daily. Were we doing something wrong? Then the pandemic shut us down.

    5M+ cups sold


    During lockdown, we perfected our product and positioning. Since our launch in early 2021, Tigglers have shown us immense love. We deliver happiness daily. üôà
